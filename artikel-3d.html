3D Room Transformation Animation

*Proof of work: 3D animation demonstration video recording.*

This project shows the transformation of a space from a messy to a tidy state using 3D animation techniques. It emphasizes asset modeling, lighting, and camera movement to produce visually appealing and satisfying content.

The animation development process involves careful keyframing techniques to ensure that each object moves naturally, as well as the use of high-quality rendering to highlight the realistic textures and atmosphere of the space.
